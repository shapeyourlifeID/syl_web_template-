About

The Government of Ireland International Education Scholarship (GOI-IES) is available for international students wishing to pursue a Master's, Postgraduate Diploma, or PhD at Irish universities. The GOI-IES program is funded by the Government of Ireland in partnership with Irish higher education institutions (HEIs) and aims to promote the internationalization of Irish education. Each year, 60 scholarships are awarded to international students who demonstrate outstanding academic performance, leadership potential, and commitment to global engagement.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be domiciled outside the EU/EEA, Switzerland, and the United Kingdom.

  • Must have a conditional or final offer of admission from a higher education institution included in the program at the time of application and submit proof of the same.

  • Applicants may submit up to three separate applications if they hold more than one offer.

  • Applicants should demonstrate strong academic performance, participation in extracurricular activities, and excellent communication skills.

How to Apply

  • Potential applicants should read the call documentation carefully to determine their eligibility.

  • Applications must be submitted via the online portal.

  • Applicants are required to upload two reference letters via the online portal.

Requirements

  • Online application

  • Offer of admission from a university participating in the GOI-IES program

  • Personal statement

  • Two references
