Brunei Darussalam Government Scholarship

Fully Funded for Diploma, Undergraduate Degree, and Postgraduate Master's Degree Program Brunei Darussalam

Andra
Published in Brunei Darussalam Government Scholarship (BDGS) | Policy | Dec 12, 2024

About

The Brunei Darussalam Government Scholarship is provided by the Brunei Darussalam Government and is a fully funded program that offers the opportunity to pursue a Diploma, Undergraduate degree, or Postgraduate Master’s degree. It is open to international students who intend to pursue undergraduate and masters studies at Universiti Brunei Darussalam (UBD), Universiti Islam Sultan Sharif Ali (UNISSA), Universiti Teknologi Brunei (UTB) and Politeknik Brunei (PB) of Brunei Darussalam.

Estimated Application Duration

January - 15 February

Benefits

  • Exemption of tuition fees including acceptance, examination, registration, and orientation fees for the duration of the program

  • Roundway economy class air ticket

  • Monthly personal allowance of BND$500.00

  • Monthly meal allowance of BND$150.00

  • An annual book allowance of BND$600.00

  • Accommodation at the residential college of the University/ Polytechnic

  • Baggage allowance for shipment

  • Insurance for the whole duration of the Scholarship

How to Apply

  • Fill out application forms from online scholarship portal

  • Students applying to Universiti Brunei Darussalam must also complete an online application through Universiti Brunei Darussalam Online Admission System

  • Application forms must be duly completed and endorsed by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs or the National Focal Point for scholarships of the applicant's country.

  • Applicants are required to submit a security clearance statement from their National Security Agency(ies)/ Police Station (i.e. a statement/ report certifying that applicants are clear from any civil and criminal charges).

  • Completed application forms are to be emailed to the following address: applybdgs2022@mfa.gov.bn

  • There may be an interview requirement and a written examination to measure the applicant's proficiency in English/ Malay/ Arabic language (where applicable) as determined by the institution offering the program

Requirements

  • Online application (students who apply to Universiti Brunei need to fill additional online form)

  • Certified true copies of academic qualifications and other supporting documents (i.e. certificates, testimonials, transcripts).

  • Certified English translations of supporting documents must be submitted for documents that are not in English.

  • Applicant’s Statement of Purpose

  • Certified true copies of Birth Certificate and Passport.

  • Security Vetting from country of origin/residence.
